Directions

To get from Milwaukee, Wisconsin to Port Washington, Wisconsin, start by driving north on I-43. After about 30 miles, take exit 95 for WI-33 toward Port Washington/Grafton. Turn left onto WI-33 and continue for about 4 miles. Turn left onto N Franklin St and continue into downtown Port Washington.
